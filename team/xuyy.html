About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate at the School of Information Science and Technology at ShanghaiTech University, supervised by Prof. Shenghua Gao. My research interest focuses on image and video understanding, visual attention prediction. Especially, my research mainly focuses on personalized saliency detection, gaze prediction and saliency detection in 360-degree videos. Before coming to ShanghaiTech University, I received a BEng. Degree from Dalian University of Technology.
